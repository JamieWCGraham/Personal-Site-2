Hi, I'm Jamie Graham. I'm a singer-songwriter, producer, pianist, and guitarist based in Toronto, Ontario. I'm currently working on my debut album 'Hurricane Violet' which is due for release this summer. Some of my influences are Joni Mitchell, Damien Rice, Frank Zappa, John Mayer, Elvis Costello, Purple Mountains, and Alex Cameron.
